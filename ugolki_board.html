The Rules of the Game
The game starts with the two sets of pieces placed in opposing corners. The aim for both players is to move their pieces into the opponents corner, using the rules below.
Player's can move their pieces left, right, forwards and backwards.
Player's can also jump their pieces over other pieces on the board to move further in one turn.
when playing the game all possible moves will be highlighted when a piece is selected. to deselect a piece click it a second time.
